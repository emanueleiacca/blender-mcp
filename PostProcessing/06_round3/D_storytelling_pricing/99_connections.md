# 99 — Connections: come questo round si lega a R1, R2 e R3

**Scopo**: ogni elemento R3-D (storytelling/pricing/foto/listing/community) è **utile solo se ancorato** al prodotto fisico (tecnica + tool + sourcing) descritto in R1, R2, R3-A/B/C. Questo file mappa esplicitamente le connessioni in modo da poter costruire un "package coerente" SKU-per-SKU.

## 11. Schema visuale: 1 SKU → cluster completo

```
[1 PEZZO R3-B marmorino 12 cm]
  ↓
[R2-E workflow porcellana economy 50 min, €0.55 materiali]
  + [R3-B marmorino layer techinique +30 min, €0.15 materiali]
  + [R3-D narrative "Cinquecento veneziano"]
  ↓
[FOTO 6-shot R3-D §3 setup R3-C light box]
  ↓
[VIDEO reel R3-D §4 time-lapse processo, riusa setup]
  ↓
[LISTING Etsy R3-D §5: title + 13 tag + description + 10 foto + video]
  ↓
[PACKAGING R3-D §6: sigillo wax + thank-you + care card] €1.45/pezzo
  ↓
[PRICING R3-D §2: 80 € retail standard, 130 € premium edition 25 numerata]
  ↓
[COMMUNITY R3-D §7: reel IG/TikTok + Pinterest + email drop]
  ↓
[ANALYTICS R3-D §8: A/B thumbnail, prezzo, bundle]
  ↓
[VENDITA → packaging → unboxing → review → UGC → next cycle]
```

**Investimento totale setup una tantum**: ~€100 (tool R2-F + light box R3-C + sigillo wax + cartoncini + cutter).
**Tempo per pezzo (post-setup)**: 80 min produzione + 8 min foto + 5 min listing + 5 min packaging = **~100 min**.
**Margine target**: cost full burden 30 € / retail 80 € = **62% margine** (lotto 25).

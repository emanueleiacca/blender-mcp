## UV protection PLA — scientific

- **Copinet, A., Bertrand, C., Govindin, S., Coma, V., Couturier, Y. (2004)**. *Chemosphere* 55(5). Effects of UV (315 nm), temperature, RH on PLA film degradation.
- **Rasselet, D., Ruellan, A., Guinault, A., Miquelard-Garnier, G., Sollogoub, C., Fayolle, B. (2014)**. *European Polymer Journal* 50. Oxidative degradation of PLA.
- **Bocchini, S., Fukushima, K., Di Blasio, A., Fina, A., Frache, A., Geobaldo, F. (2010)**. *Biomacromolecules* / *Polymer Degradation and Stability* 95. PLA photooxidation FTIR.
- **Sakai, W. et al. (2004–2006)** — *Polymer Degradation and Stability*, fotodegradazione PLA.
- **MDPI Polymers** journal — open access, query "PLA UV degradation", "PLA photostability".
- **Journal of Applied Polymer Science** — review biopolymer outdoor durability.
- Google Scholar queries suggerite: `"PLA" UV degradation outdoor`, `polylactic acid photodegradation HALS`, `PLA UV stabilizer benzotriazole`.
